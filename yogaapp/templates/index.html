Yoga live classes

Beginner
A beginner's yoga class offers a welcoming environment for those new to the practice. The instructor guides participants through basic poses and breathing techniques, ensuring everyone feels comfortable and supported. It's a great way to improve flexibility, reduce stress, and start a journey towards overall wellness.

Hatha
A Hatha yoga class focuses on balancing the mind and body through a combination of physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation. It typically involves holding poses for extended periods to build strength and flexibility while promoting relaxation. Participants often leave feeling rejuvenated, centered, and more aware of their physical and mental states.

Vinyasa
A Vinyasa yoga class typically involves smoothly transitioning from one pose to another in a flowing sequence, synchronized with the breath. It is known for its dynamic and invigorating style, which can be physically challenging yet deeply rewarding. Participants often experience improved flexibility, strength, and mental clarity through regular practice.

Ashtanga
Ashtanga yoga class is a dynamic and physically demanding practice that follows a specific sequence of postures. It emphasizes the synchronization of breath with movement to create a flowing and meditative experience. This practice is known for building strength, flexibility, and mental clarity.
